Precautions For Hepatitis-B

To avoid getting or spreading the virus to others:

  • Don't share needles.
  • Wear latex or plastic gloves if you have touch bloods.
  • Use a condom when you have a sex.
  • Don't share toothbrushes or razors.
  • Don't get a tattoo,or make sure that the needles used have been cleaned properly and are sterilised.
  • The hepatitis-B vaccine is the best way to prevent infection.
