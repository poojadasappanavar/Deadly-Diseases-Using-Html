Precautions For Cancer

  • Try yoga or acupunture .studies have shown that they may help to relieve cancer fatigue.
  • Rest.Take 20 min naps or breaks during day to regain your strength
  • Eat well.Focus on a balanced diet with lots of fresh vegetables and fruits.If you dont feel hungry or it's hard to eat,talk to dietitian to see how you can you get nutrients you need.
  • Check your medicines.Some drugs you take to treat cancer can make you sleepy.Ask your doctor if you can change medicines or adjust the dose.
  • Exercise.Moving actually gives you more energy than staying on the couch.Get in the habit of taking daily walk for 15 min.you will get stronger and feel more alert
